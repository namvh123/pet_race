How to play?

  • Step 1: Tap on button Start game down below
  • Step 2: Choose your Pet that you want to bring along. The Pet with better attributes will have an advantage
  • Step 3: Select your desired mode then tap Find match to start. Don’t forget to read the rule of each mode
  • Step 4: Wait for your opponents. It might take a while
  • Step 5: Cheer you Pet on !!! You will gain Elo point and reward for each victory.
  • Step 6: Strive to improve your pet and accumulate as many achievements as possible. Top 5 best players will receive reward from the Jackpot pool at the end of the season.
    DON’T MISS !!

What is Jackpot?

Jack pot is the reward for the top 5 best players of the current season. For each paid match, a portion of total amount will be subtracted and put into the jackpot pool.
Important note: Once Jackpot is awarded, the Leaderboard will automatically reset to begin another season. At that exact time, total Elo points of each player will be reset.

Is the Jackpot worth it?

Many players will be honored on Jackpot Leaderboard, but ony 5 best of them-who have the highest Elo point- stand a chance to win. The prize is unlimited and distributed as follow:

  • Top 1: 50%

  • Top 2: 20%

  • Top 3: 15%

  • Top 4: 10%

  • Top 5: 5%

The winners and prize value is determined and immediately sent to winners’s Speding wallet after the season is over.
